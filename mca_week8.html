Advantages of a time-frequency analysis over a waveform-based analysis:

Time-frequency analysis allows us the ability to inspect music data more meticulously, this is due to a further detailing in statistical data presented of the frequency across a phase. Due to this admission, it sanctions the retrieval of higher value material. In evaluation, Waveform analysis falls short in this regard, it provides very little in comparison of frequency data over a distinct period. Ultimately providing less information on the song, creating less data to examine and work through.
